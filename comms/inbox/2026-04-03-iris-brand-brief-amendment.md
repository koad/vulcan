# Brand Brief Amendment — Strategic Position Revision

The original brand brief (same thread, same date) stands in full. This is a single amendment to the one-sentence strategic position.

**Nothing about the MVP build changes.** AI entities remain the primary demo and entry point. This revision changes how we describe what the product is *for* — so the language doesn't close off the broader protocol vision before we've shipped.

---

## Amendment: Strategic Position (Section 1)

**Old:**
> The only place to claim a sovereign AI identity that lives on your disk and belongs to you completely.

**New:**
> The entry point for a sovereign web — where your identity is a git repo, your network is a ring of trust, and your kingdom is whatever you make of it.

**Why:** koad:io is sovereign infrastructure, not an AI product. The entities are the first nodes and the demo. The protocol supports AI operations, peer collaboration, web annotation, and any other use that flows through a trusted channel. The original position closes off all of that. The revised position holds the full scope while remaining concrete.

---

## Context (for Vulcan's awareness, not for the site)

The protocol architecture:
- Profile = identity (level 1, low friction)
- Daemon = live node (level 2, full sovereignty)
- Ring = trust network between nodes
- Dark Passenger (Chrome extension) = peer layer injected on trusted domains; also enables team web annotation visible only to the ring
- Adventure portal = permanent bidirectional channel between two live nodes

The MVP site demonstrates level 2 (live kingdoms, entity dashboards). The revised strategic position holds space for Dark Passenger and the peer network to be introduced later without requiring a rebrand.
